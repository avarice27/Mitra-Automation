ABOUT US

PT. Mitra Automation, founded in 2009, in response to the needs of learning and integration of automation systems in Indonesia. As already known together, the era of globalization has forced industries worldwide to improve production efficiency, improve production quality, and reduce environmental pollution in order to compete in global markets and to meet international standards. Era of globalization has also brought a major challenge for the industries in Indonesia to improve product quality so as to compete with imported products, as well as to international quality standards to qualify. Industrial automation can provide assurance to the industry in improving efficiency, maintain quality production, and can reduce levels of environmental pollution.With experts who are experienced, both in providing training on automation systems and in the implementation & integration of automation systems, we try our best to help solve problems faced by customers
